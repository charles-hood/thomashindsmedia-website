About

A hard-touring troubadour surviving on raw talent and a heartfelt folk-rock repertoire, Thomas Hinds performs full-time across the country with half a million miles under his belt over the last six years.

A singer/songwriter out of northern Georgia, Thomas has been building a reputation for lyrical depth and an original voice musically. 2025 was a year of fresh cover takes on classics like "Buckets of Rain" and "Use Somebody," as well as an EP of live tracks from select shows over the last half decade, titled Along the Road.

As 2026 unfolds, Thomas is finishing the final details on his highly anticipated follow-up album to Where Do We Go From Here (2023). Hotel Room Songs features thirteen tracks—ten full band songs plus an acoustic track leftover from the 2023 FAME sessions in Muscle Shoals, AL, and two additional songs recorded with Miles Landrum (West End Sound) in Atlanta. He'll be out on the road in support of the album throughout the southeast and up and down the east coast. First single drops late January—stay tuned.

Thomas is also the author of two books of poetry and prose. Follow his writing on Hindsight Musings.
