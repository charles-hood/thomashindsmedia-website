About

A hard-touring troubadour surviving on raw talent and a heartfelt folk-rock repertoire, Thomas Hinds performs full-time across the country with half a million miles under his belt over the last six years.

A singer/songwriter out of northern Georgia, Thomas has been building a reputation for lyrical depth and an original voice. His 2023 album Where Do We Go From Here, recorded at Alabama's legendary FAME Studios, reached #86 on the Americana Album charts.

His highly anticipated follow-up, Hotel Room Songs, drops in 2026 featuring thirteen tracks recorded at FAME Studios and West End Sound in Atlanta. First single coming late January.
