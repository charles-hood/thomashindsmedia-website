Timing

Set Length: 45-90 minutes (negotiable)

Setup: 15-20 minutes

Soundcheck: 20-30 minutes preferred

Sound Requirements

Vocals

  • Quality vocal microphone on boom stand
  • Preferred: Shure SM58, Sennheiser e835
  • Condenser acceptable for quiet rooms

Acoustic Guitar

  • Active DI box for guitar pickup
  • Preferred: Radial J48, Countryman Type 85
  • Guitar cable provided by artist

Monitors

  • One floor wedge monitor
  • Mix: vocals prominent, guitar for pitch
  • IEM not required but can accommodate

Stage

  • Minimum 8' x 8' performance area
  • Stool or armless chair
  • Small table for water/setlist
  • Guitar stand
