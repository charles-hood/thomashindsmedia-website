Thomas Hinds

Solo Acoustic Artist & Poetic Troubadour

A hard-touring troubadour from northern Georgia, Thomas Hinds performs full-time across the country, averaging 70,000 miles annually. His sound has been described as "a cross between the ethereal crooning of Jeff Buckley and the down-to-earth angst of early Wilco." His 2023 album Where Do We Go From Here, recorded at Alabama's legendary FAME Studios, reached #86 on the Americana Album charts.
