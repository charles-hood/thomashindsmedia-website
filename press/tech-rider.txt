THOMAS HINDS
Technical Rider & Stage Requirements

================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Thomas Hinds is a touring troubadour with a flexible setup designed to work
in venues of all sizes, from intimate listening rooms to larger stages.

PERFORMANCE CONFIGURATIONS:
- Solo acoustic (most common)
- Duo
- Trio
- Full band

This rider covers the solo acoustic setup. For duo, trio, or full band
configurations, please contact thomashindsschedule@gmail.com to discuss
specific requirements.

Performance Duration: Typically 45-90 minutes (negotiable)
Setup Time: 15-20 minutes (solo); varies for larger configurations
Soundcheck: 20-30 minutes preferred

================================================================================
STAGE REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

MINIMUM STAGE AREA
- 8' x 8' performance area
- Stool or chair (armless preferred)
- Small table or stand for water, setlist, capo

LIGHTING
- Basic stage wash adequate for most performances
- Avoid heavy backlighting that obscures performer from audience
- Warm tones preferred if color options available

================================================================================
SOUND REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

VOCALS
- (1) Quality vocal microphone on boom stand
  Preferred: Shure SM58, Sennheiser e835, or similar dynamic mic
  Acceptable: Condenser mic for quieter listening room settings
- Mic stand: Boom stand, adjustable height

ACOUSTIC GUITAR
- (1) DI Box for acoustic guitar pickup
  Preferred: Radial J48, Countryman Type 85, or similar active DI
- Guitar cable provided by artist
- Guitar stand

MONITORS
- (1) Floor wedge monitor with vocal and guitar mix
- Monitor mix: Vocals prominent, guitar for pitch reference
- In-ear monitoring NOT required but can be accommodated

BACKLINE
================================================================================

Artist provides:
- Acoustic guitar(s)
- Guitar cables
- Capos, picks, spare strings
- Tuner

Venue provides:
- PA system appropriate for room size
- Vocal microphone and stand
- DI box for guitar
- Monitor wedge
- Basic stage lighting

================================================================================
HOSPITALITY (APPRECIATED BUT NOT REQUIRED)
================================================================================

- Room temperature bottled water on stage
- Light meal or buyout for performances over 2 hours from lodging
- Safe, secure area for instrument storage during load-in
